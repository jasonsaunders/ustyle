All styles of imagery and photography should uphold the brand values where applicable. Frank, human, clear, confident, possable.

Lifestyle photography

Our lifestyle and people-focused imagery should reassert the brand values of fairness, clarity and confidence. The images should have a British feel where possible, avoiding obviously American or global images. Images should be ‘reportage’ in style, avoiding cheesy, posed, forced or unrealistic photography.
