Images Github: _images.scss

Helpful image classes for making images full width or floating within content.

  • .us-img--full - Full width image
  • .us-img--left - Left floated image
  • .us-img--right - Right floated image

Responsive Github: _responsive.scss

Utility classes to allow display of elements across devices. You have the option of displaying them as block, inline-block or inline elements. You can also hide elements by .us-{DEVICE}--hidden and will hide those elements above that breakpoint.

Example

Shows large desktop and above
Shows desktop and above
Shows tablet and above
Shows small tablet and above
Shows mobile and above
Hides from tablet and above

                 <div class='us-lg-desktop--block'>Shows large desktop and above</div>
                 <div class='us-desktop--block'>Shows desktop and above</div>
                 <div class='us-tablet--block'>Shows tablet and above</div>
                 <div class='us-sm-tablet--block'>Shows small tablet and above</div>
                 <div class='us-mobile-block'>Shows mobile and above</div>
                 <div class='us-tablet--hidden'>Hides from tablet and above</div>
