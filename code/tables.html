USPs

USPs Github: _usp.scss

USPs (Unique Selling Points) are used within tables and on splash pages to highlight offers within a deal, and also to differentiate deals. General guidelines; There should only be one USP per deal. Text within USPs should be written in sentence case (not uppercase).

  • .us-usp--blue - uSwitch related
  • .us-usp--orange - Rewards (money/points)
  • .us-usp--purple - Gifts eg. free TV
  • .us-usp--yellow - Vouchers
  • .us-usp--typecyan - Micro persuasions eg. most popular, half price
  • .us-usp--green - Renewable and environmentally friendly
  • .us-usp--navy - Generic vertical specific USP
  • .us-usp--cyan - Generic vertical specific USP
  • .us-usp--typegrey - Generic vertical specific USP
  • .us-usp--red - Generic vertical specific USP
